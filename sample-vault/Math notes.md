## Equations

The classic mass-energy equivalence:

$$ E = mc^2 $$

Pythagoras:

$$ c = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} $$

Quadratic formula:

$$ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} $$

A sum, with bounds:

$$ \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{k!} = e $$

Set notation:

$$ \mathbb{R} = \{ x : x \in \mathbb{Q} \cup \mathbb{Z} \} $$
