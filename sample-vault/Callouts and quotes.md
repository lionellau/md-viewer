# Callouts and quotes

## Callouts

Five types are supported. The title after the type keyword is optional.

> [!note] This is a note
> Use notes for supplementary information that's worth highlighting.

> [!tip] Pro tip
> Tips are for helpful shortcuts or non-obvious workflows.

> [!info]
> No custom title — falls back to the type name ("Info").

> [!warning] Watch out
> Warnings are for things that could go wrong if you're not careful.

> [!danger] Do not do this
> Danger is for destructive or irreversible actions.

## Blockquotes

Plain blockquotes (without the `[!type]` tag) render as standard quotes:

> The best way to predict the future is to invent it.
> — Alan Kay

Nested:

> Outer level
>
> > Inner level — nested blockquote.
